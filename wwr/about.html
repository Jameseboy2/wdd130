History

Founded in the spring of 2024, Jay's Rapid Rivers Rafting began as a small family-run operation in the heart of the Rocky Mountains. Our founder, James Taylor, a former kayaker and nature enthusiast, envisioned a company that would not only offer thrilling white water experiences but also foster a deep appreciation for the river's natural beauty and ecosystems.

With just a handful of rafts and a single riverside cabin, James and his team set out to create unforgettable adventures for every visitor. They focused on safety, fun, and education, quickly earning a reputation for their passionate guides and personalized service. Over the years, Jay's Rapid Rivers Rafting has expanded, now featuring a fleet of top-of-the-line rafts, a fully-equipped riverside lodge, and a variety of river courses ranging from serene floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids.
